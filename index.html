    Immaculate Concepcion College

    200X - 20XX

    The Immaculate Conception College (ICC) is a private, Catholic school founded in 1935 in Balayan, Batangas. It started in a convent and initially offered programs for girls at the primary level. Six years later, ICC offered programs in the intermediate and secondary level. Also a year later, undergraduate courses like Preparatory Law and Associate in Commercial Science became available.

    Batangas State Univerty

    200X - 20XX

    Established in 1994, the Batangas State University - Balayan (BatStateU - Balayan) is a government-owned academic institution that is part of the Batangas State University system. In its first years, the campus started offering courses in Welding Fabrication, Electrical, Automotive, and Electronics Technology.
